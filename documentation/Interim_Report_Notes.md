## Interim Report - Notes

### Requirements
- 3000-4000 words (6-8 pages) 
- Aim to assess and reflect on progress

Should include:
1. Project Background & Understanding (10%)
2. Requirements & Critical Analysis (15%)
3. Initial Software Implementation & Testing (30%)
4. Project Management & Progress (15%)
5. Preliminary Reflection (15%)
6. Style (8%)
7. Resource Links (2%)
8. Conclusion & Summary (5%)

### 1. Project Background & Understanding 
- Evidence of understanding of initial brief
- Awareness of problem (and existing products)

### 2. Requirements & Critical Analysis
- Coverage of requirements (use cases?)
- Analysis of hardware/tools/methods for the project (and motivations/justifications)

### 3. Initial Software Implementation & Testing
- Proof of concept implementation (pictures of designs and explanations)
- Talk about use of version control (git branching, merging, issues etc.)
- Evidence of testing and documentation (test plans, peer reviews, comments etc.)

### 4. Project Management & Progress
- Use of Jira to organise our tickets (use of story points to plan deadlines and teamwork)
- Talk about use of agile technologies and features e.g test driven development
- Use of Microsoft Teams for regular communication between individuals, subteams and the whole team (different channels for meetings, resources etc)
- Use of soft skills e.g teamwork, leadership, organisation, motivation, professionalism etc.

### 5. Preliminary Reflection
- Tools and techniques used 
- Any problems we encountered so far (getting used to test driven development etc.)
- Functioning of the team so far (success of subteams and regular meetings - ability to feedback)
